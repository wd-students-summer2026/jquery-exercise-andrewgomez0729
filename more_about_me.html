Experience One

A unique experience I had was when I went to Ecuador in 2022. This was my first time there and I was there to visit family which I have never met in person. When I was in Ecuador I went to a town called Gualaceo and saw the biggest rock I have ever seen. On top of the rock was a cross. The reason for the cross is that there is a story of a priest fighting the devil on this rock and the priest beat the devil.

Experience Two

Another unique experience I had in Ecuador was trying fruits which are not found in the U.S. One of these fruits is called guama. Guama is this large fruit which has different seeds and these seeds are wrapped in a white flesh which is the actual fruit. You eat the white flesh of the fruit and discard the seeds. This fruit has a sweet taste and it was very good.
